About Am I Cooked? — Financial Health Check

Am I Cooked? is a free financial health check tool that gives you an honest, no-nonsense assessment of your personal finances. Enter your age, annual salary, monthly rent, total savings, and total debt — and the tool calculates a financial health score from 0 to 100 using standard benchmarks like the 50/30/20 budgeting rule, recommended savings rates by age, and healthy debt-to-income ratios.

How it works: Your score weighs four factors — rent burden (how much of your income goes to housing), savings rate (what percentage you're putting away monthly), debt load (total debt relative to income), and savings benchmark (how your nest egg compares to recommended targets for your age). The result is a blunt, shareable verdict: from "Financially Thriving" all the way to "Absolutely Cooked." No financial advice, just vibes and math.
