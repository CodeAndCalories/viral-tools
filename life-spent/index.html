About Life Spent On… — Life Time Calculator

Life Spent On… is a free life time calculator that converts your daily habits into something visceral: years of your life. Enter your age and how many hours per day you spend on activities like sleeping, scrolling social media, commuting, watching TV, or eating — and the tool converts those hours into total years spent since you were 5 years old.

How it works: For each activity, your daily hours are multiplied by your total days lived (from age 5 to now). The results are displayed as years, months, and days — ranked from biggest time-sink to smallest. The tool also provides context comparisons to make the numbers hit harder. Everything runs in your browser privately, and you can share your exact results via a unique link that encodes your inputs.
