About VibeCheck

VibeCheck is a collection of free, privacy-first web tools built for real everyday decisions. Every tool runs entirely in your browser — no accounts, no servers, no data collected. Just genuinely useful tools with zero friction.

Current tools include: the Am I Cooked? financial health checker that scores your finances 0–100 based on income, rent, savings, and debt; the What Should I Eat? meal picker that suggests perfect meals with full recipes based on your mood and diet; the Life Spent On… time calculator that converts your daily habits into haunting years-of-your-life stats; and the AI Pickup Line Generator with 100+ personalized lines across categories.
